Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a solar installation take?

Most residential installations are completed within 2-3 days. However, the entire process including planning, permits, and inspections typically takes 4-8 weeks depending on your location and local regulations.

What is the average payback period for a solar investment?

The average payback period for residential solar systems is 5-7 years. Commercial systems often see a return on investment in 4-6 years thanks to various incentives and tax benefits.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans to ensure your system operates at peak efficiency. Most systems require minimal maintenance, primarily occasional cleaning and annual inspections.

What happens during power outages?

With a standard grid-tied system, your solar panels will shut off during a power outage for safety reasons. However, if you add battery storage to your system, you can continue to power your home or business during outages.
